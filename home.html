ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to Hang A Pisayan! We've reimagined the classic "Hangman" specifically for the Pisay community.


In our version, we've traded the traditional gallows for the academic grind. "Hanging" here represents the struggle to stay afloat during exam season. We've replaced generic words with Science and Math terminology to keep your brain sharp!


Pass the "exam" by guessing the word to earn your digital Student ID. Fail, and well... there's always the next retake!
